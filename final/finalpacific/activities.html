Activities at Pacific Trails

Hiking

Pacific Trails Resort has 5 miles of hiking and is adjacent to a state park. Go alone or join one of our guided hikes.

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are available for guest use.

Bird Watching

While anytime is a good time for bird watching at Pacific, we offer guided bird watching trips at sunrise several times a week.
